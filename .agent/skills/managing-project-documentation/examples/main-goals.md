# Admin Panel Main Goals

## Objective
The Helping Hand Trust administrative portal is designed to provide complete operational oversight of all charitable activities, vendor distributions, and donor interactions. 

## Business Requirements
- **Simplicity & Speed:** Volunteers should be able to process Zakat orders instantly.
- **Data Integrity:** Strict role separation and complete audit trails for every donation.
- **Unified Design:** Ensure a consistent "Midnight Emerald" UI tone that looks premium and instills trust.
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first approach for SPOCs working on the ground.

## Future Ambitions
- Expand the portal to include non-zakat fundraising avenues.
- AI integration for automatic receipt parsing and donor communication via WhatsApp.
